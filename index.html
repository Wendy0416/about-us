关于我们

成立于1925年的故宫博物院，建立在明清两朝皇宫——紫禁城的基础上。 历经六百年兴衰荣辱，帝王宫殿的大门终于向公众敞开。故宫博物院拥有1863404件藏品，是世界上规模最大，保存最完整的木结构宫殿建筑群.
故宫博物院是中华民族的骄傲所在，也是全人类的珍贵文化遗产。如今，昔日皇宫禁地那占地112万平方米的重重宫阙，既是收藏明清皇室珍宝的巨大宝库，也是记载明清宫廷历史的鲜活档案……

The Palace Museum, established in 1925, is based on the Forbidden City, the imperial palace of the Ming and Qing dynasties. After six hundred years of ups and downs, the gate of the imperial palace was finally opened to the public. With 1,863,404 collections, the Palace Museum is the largest and most well-preserved wooden palace complex in the world.
The Palace Museum is the pride of the Chinese nation and a precious cultural heritage of all mankind. Today, the former imperial palace forbidden area covers an area of ​​1.12 million square meters. It is not only a huge treasure house for the collection of Ming and Qing imperial treasures, but also a live archive of the history of the Ming and Qing courts...
